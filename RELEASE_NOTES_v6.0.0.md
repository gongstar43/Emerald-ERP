## 🌟 **MAJOR FEATURES**

### 🌍 **1. Multi-Country Legal System (5 Countries)**

Complete integration of legal systems for 5 Arab countries with automatic application based on company settings.

#### **Supported Countries:**

| Country | Flag | Labor Law | Tax Law | Companies Law | Currency |
|---------|------|-----------|---------|---------------|----------|
| **Iraq** | 🇮🇶 | Law 37/2015 | Law 113/1982 | Law 21/1997 | IQD |
| **Saudi Arabia** | 🇸🇦 | Royal Decree M/51 | VAT & Income Tax | Royal Decree M/132 | SAR |
| **UAE** | 🇦🇪 | Law 33/2021 | Corporate Tax 47/2022 | Law 32/2021 | AED |
| **Egypt** | 🇪🇬 | Law 12/2003 | Law 91/2005 | Law 159/1981 | EGP |
| **Jordan** | 🇯🇴 | Law 8/1996 | Law 34/2014 | Law 22/1997 | JOD |

#### **Features:**
- ✅ **15 Complete Laws** (5 Labor + 5 Tax + 5 Companies)
- ✅ **Automatic Law Application** based on country selection
- ✅ **Accurate Calculations** for taxes and end-of-service gratuities
- ✅ **Updated Information** (2023-2024)
- ✅ **Dynamic Salary & Tax Calculations**

#### **Implementation:**
```typescript

// Laws automatically applied to:
- HR > Payroll
- HR > End of Service
- Accounting > Taxes
- All financial calculations
```
